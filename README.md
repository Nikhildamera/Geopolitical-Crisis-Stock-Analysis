🚨 US-Iran War 2026 — Stock Market Crisis Analysis

A real-world Excel financial analysis project examining how the US-Iran War (March 2026) crashed global stock markets — identifying winners, losers, and key investment lessons.


📌 Project Overview
When war broke out between the US and Iran on March 3, 2026, global markets were thrown into chaos. The S&P 500 wiped all its yearly gains in a single month. Oil hit $120 per barrel. Tech stocks crashed while Defence and Energy stocks surged.
This project analyses 5 major US stocks across 4 contrasting sectors to understand exactly what happened — and why.

📚 Lesson: Diversification across sectors protects a portfolio


🧠 What I Learned
This project taught me that markets don't crash equally — during the US-Iran War:

Losers: Technology, Airlines, Semiconductors
Winners: Energy, Defence, Agriculture
Safe Havens: Consumer Staples, US Dollar

The key concept is called Sector Rotation — professional investors move money between sectors based on economic conditions. Understanding this is what separates informed investors from everyone else.
